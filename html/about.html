My Poetry

Think about the time you are separated from someone. 
 
		
	The star

	Last night, 
	when the entire world was in silence,
	I looked up the sky.
	There was only one star accompanying me,
	to endure the darkness and coldness.

	Tonight,
	the sky is teeming with stars,
	the night becomes gentle and warm.

	But where are you, my star?

	You are submerged into the milky way,
	and also cannnot find me out of crowds.
