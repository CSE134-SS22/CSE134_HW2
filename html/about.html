My Poetry

Sometimes you miss someone because you were not brave enough 
 
		
	Miss the dancing with rain

	Rain gently sweeps our city 
	which was hot and dry;
	however, during rain nights I rarely go out 
	to feel it 
	because I fear the coldness
	soon the rain ends 
	and what follows is long, boring summer 
	I regret that I did not go out to dance with the rain.
